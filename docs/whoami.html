Who is JustG.io

A little description of me

I am Gio, a 22 year old Italian guy, I am a software engineer with a degree in computer science. In addition to computer science, which takes me away 8 hours a day with my job, I have many other passions, including: investments, sports, trekking and (like everybody) traveling. I created this site with the intent to keep track of everything I do and face in my life, I thought maybe it could be interesting for someone. I have no expectations in this project I don't give myself goals on numbers to reach, I do it for personal passion and to force myself to keep track of my progress related to all my projects and my personal growth.
